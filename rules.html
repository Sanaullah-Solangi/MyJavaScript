  6. kisi bhi storage men koi cheez store kese ki jae ya asign ki jae iska kya rule hai

    JavaScript ka ye rule bhi yaad rakhen k jab bhi or khin bhi esa ho k equal to " = " ka sign laga ho or uske left or right side per kuch values hun to iska mtlab ye hoga k right side wali value left side wali value yani storage men asign hogi.
    ab left side men kuch bhi esa ho skta hai jis men values store ki ja skti hen jese "variable", "array", "object" ya "function" in men se kisi men bhi koi bhi value store ho skti hai or right side men bhi koi bhi value ho skti hai wo "string", "varibale", "object", "array", "function" ya kuch bhi ho skta hai. for example.
    fullName = "sanaullah"
